Mobile Legend

Mobile Legends is a fast-paced Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile devices. Players control a unique hero with special abilities and compete in teams to destroy the opposing team's base while defending their own. The game emphasizes strategic gameplay, teamwork, and quick reflexes.

CLASH OF CLANS

Clash of Clans is a popular mobile strategy game where players build and upgrade their villages, train armies, and raid other players for resources. The game features a mix of base building, resource management, and strategic combat, with players forming clans to collaborate in battles and complete challenges.

COUNTER-Strike

Counter-Strike is a legendary first-person shooter (FPS) franchise that emphasizes tactical gameplay and teamwork. Players assume the role of either terrorists or counter-terrorists, engaging in objective-based missions like bomb defusal or hostage rescue. Known for its competitive multiplayer scene, it shaped the esports landscape.
